Khlo Faces Her Fear of Whales in New Kardashians Season 4 Clip

 Posted on September 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  353 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The Kardashians are back with an all new season, and they’re already giving us major drama – this time centering around Khloé Kardashian’s intense fear of whales.

A newly released clip from the Season 4 premiere episode shows the family vacationing in Cabo during whale watching season. While her sisters anticipate seeing the majestic sea creatures, a squeamish Khloé makes it clear she wants no part of the ocean action.

Khloé’s Extreme Whale Phobia

In the clip, Khloé is visibly uncomfortable when she learns whales may swim close to shore. She tells her family “I feel like I’m gonna cry” at the thought of seeing one.

The Good American founder goes so far as to grab binoculars to scan for whales from her balcony. When one inevitably surfaces, she screams and cowers in fear.

Later in a confessional, Khloé traces her terror back to a traumatic Snapple fact she read about a couple being swallowed by a whale. She calls the ocean giants “sick” and wants nothing to do with them.

Momager Kris Jenner also weighs in, apologizing for whatever childhood incident sparked this deep-seated phobia. It’s a dramatic, hilarious focus for the season premiere.

More Mexico Antics

Beyond Khloé’s whale meltdown, the Mexico trip also features food fights, beach volleyball, and Kendall Jenner judging a cooking competition.

The tropical getaway promises laughs, bonding, and the signature Kardashian vacation antics fans love. Of course, relationship drama also arises amidst the fun in the sun.

What’s Next for Season 4?

While the premiere delivers the entertainment we expect from the famous family, Season 4 also promises deeper revelations.

The Kardashians continue to open up about their lives in even more intimate ways this season. Viewers get an inside look at pivotal moments like Kim pursuing a new career path.

After 14 years on air, the Kardashians still reveal shocking new sides of themselves. That’s the secret to this franchise’s unmatched longevity.

So get ready to see Khloé’s whale meltdown and much more when Season 4 Ep-1 premieres September 28 on Hulu. One thing’s for sure – it’s going to be a wild ride!
