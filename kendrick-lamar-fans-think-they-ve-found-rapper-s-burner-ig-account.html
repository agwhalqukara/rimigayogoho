 Posted on September 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  319 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Kendrick Lamar fans have allegedly found the rapper’s burner account on Instagram under the alias of Jojoruski.

The Jojoruski account was previously private until earlier this week and has since compiled over 250,000 followers and counting.

Outside of the apple juice profile picture, the account features an array of Kendrick selfies, inspirational figures such as Michael Jackson and 2Pac, as well as a clip of the former TDE rapper feeding his baby daughter.

There’s even footage of Kendrick playing basketball and lighting up fireworks in the middle of the street. Check out some of the post’s below.

Fans were loving the content and getting a peak into K. Dot’s lifestyle, who is usually very keen on staying out of the public eye.

“Look at Kendrick being normal outside the fame,” one fan wrote, while another chimed in: “At the end day, Kendrick is a normal human being!”

Harlow spoke about what it meant to receive praise from K. Dot in his Rap Radar interview earlier this week.
