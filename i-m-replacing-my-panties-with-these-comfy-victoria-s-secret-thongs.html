Im Replacing My Panties With These Comfy Victorias Secret Thongs

 Posted on August 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  419 words  |  Martina Birk

Getting your first thong is almost like a right of passage, and I was bestowed my first pair at age 16. To prevent me from having hard lines under my form-fitting formal dress, my mom gifted me a whole pack of string thongs for the occasion. While I couldn’t get over how seamless my figure looked, I was also extremely uncomfortable that entire night because my thong kept riding up. From that day forward, I stuck to bikini and brief cuts, but recently, I decided to give them another chance. I bought a pack of Victoria's Secret Pink No-Show Thong Panties, and now I understand why this underwear style is a go-to for a lot of people: You forget you even have them on.

Ranging in sizes XS to XXL, they come in a pack of five thongs, each with a mix of colors like navy, black, white, burgundy, pink, and gray. A four-way stretch fabric hugs the bum and is moisture-wicking, so you stay dry all day, while a cotton-lined gusset is comfortable and breathable. The waistband and leg holes have a cheeky raw-cut finish that looks invisible under fitted clothing and a minimal, low-rise design pairs well with low-rise bottoms.

Victoria's Secret Pink No-Show Thong Panties

I never thought I’d be reaching for a thong as my daily underwear choice, but these Pink no-shows are just as comfortable as my full-coverage styles. I don’t feel like I’m walking around with a wedgie all day, plus the minimal coverage and seamless design mean they look virtually non-existent under fitted dresses or skirts. They feel like I have nothing on, and the cotton lining makes them super breathable, so I feel fresh and dry all day. The fabric is so soft and lightweight on the skin, I feel like I’m going commando. They’re also incredibly durable and have held up well through multiple washes without warping — even without separating them into a garment bag.  

They have a snug-but-comfortable fit that’s forgiving, despite my fluctuating weight, and they lay completely flat on my skin without rolling. I even put these VS panties to the ultimate test and wore them during one of my high-intensity workouts. They didn’t move, appeared seamless under my skin-like leggings, and kept me dry despite my sweat sesh. To say I was impressed would be an understatement.
